#  Business Sales Performance Analysis  
### Data Science & Analytics Internship – Future Interns  
**Author:** Kumar Ansh Prasad  

---

##  Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, category performance, and regional profitability.  
The goal is to generate actionable business insights and strategic recommendations to improve profitability and growth.

---

##  Tools & Technologies Used
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

##  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Revenue:** $2,297,200.86  
- **Total Profit:** $28,637.02  
- **Total Orders:** 5009  
- **Average Order Value:** $458.61  

---

##  Analysis Performed

- Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis  
- Top 10 Products by Revenue & Profit  
- Category-wise Performance Analysis  
- Regional Sales & Profit Margin Evaluation  

---

##  Key Insights

1. The West region generates the highest revenue and profit.
2. The East region also performs strongly with solid profit margins.
3. The Central region has comparatively lower profit margins (~7.9%).
4. Revenue shows seasonal spikes toward year-end months.
5. Profitability varies significantly across categories and regions.

---

##  Strategic Recommendations

1. Expand focus in high-performing regions (West & East).
2. Optimize pricing strategies for low-margin segments.
3. Leverage seasonal demand trends for targeted marketing campaigns.
4. Improve cost efficiency in underperforming regions.
5. Promote high-margin products more aggressively.
